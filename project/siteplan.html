Healthy Recipes

A friendly and colorful guide to delicious, nutritious meals.

Site Name

Healthy Recipes – I chose this name because it’s simple, clear, and reflects the purpose of the website: to inspire people to cook wholesome meals at home. It’s easy to remember and directly conveys what the site offers.

Optional domain: healthyrecipeshub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a collection of nutritious, easy-to-follow recipes that promote healthy living. Visitors will find step-by-step guides, nutritional tips, and cooking inspiration. The site aims to encourage balanced eating with ingredients that are fresh, accessible, and affordable.

Scenarios

  • “I’m new to cooking—what are some simple, healthy dinner recipes I can start with?”
  • “What are some affordable, nutritious meals I can make in under 30 minutes?”
  • “Can I find vegetarian or gluten-free options on this site?”

Color Schema

Primary Color: #ffb347 – used for buttons, headings, and accent highlights to create warmth and friendliness.

Secondary Color: #77dd77 – used for titles, dividers, and visual freshness representing healthy food and nature.

The siteplan document uses these colors for headings and accents as shown here.

Typography

Font: Poppins (from Google Fonts)

  • Headings (h1, h2): Poppins, bold, green accent color.
  • Body text: Poppins, regular weight for easy reading.

This document uses the same font style for consistency.
